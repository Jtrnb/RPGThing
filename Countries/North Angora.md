History:
- Founded 503 PR (Post Rupture)


Angora was once a large hub for life, its prime being 32 BR, in a so called "golden age" of resource. The hub of technological advance, and seemingly endless agricultural resource. Known for corn oil, the first oil motor was built here pushing Angora into a dominant position for global influence.

After the Rupture Angora lost almost everything. A Fault Line split the near center of Angora causing a magical divide between the industrial and agricultural. During the time, the leaders of Angora attempted to maintain calm and peace through near martial law, keeping people inside and preventing immigration. 

Magic ruined what remained of Angora after the Rupture. The southern industrial nation climbed back to function significantly faster than the north, harnessing magic to make up for their lack of natural resources. The north however, with its lacking of technological prowess and formal education, magic became more of a weapon than a tool. 82 PR a document would be signed into law for the legal secession of the southern half of Angora, the northern left under the rule of a minor politician who would do little but maintain the status quo. 

When the Rupture Beasts began to arrive North Angora did surprisingly well. Their practice of unconventional magic paired with a rougher lifestyle and ability to live off scraps kept them alive better than most. 

While North Angora remains a nation today its often seen as a failure of the inability to use magic properly, with various nation airdropping criminals into its borders as punishment. Most of North Angora is overgrown and traditional society has collapsed. Many people there life off barter while keeping an off grid lifestyle. Many of the mages within North Angora have become Reclaimed to a point of either becoming monsters or death.
