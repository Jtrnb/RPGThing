### Year: 0 NTR

In an attempt to reach beyond lightspeed travel scientists ripped a tear into reality. Upon this crack forming within an instant 2 people in the room began confusing as if something had possessed them. The lab status report states: "Dr. Ranval and Dr Lavos started having seizure like symptoms, their bodies shaking and convulsing."

Within the next 24 hours, both patients began to show other side effects including glowing and telekinesis. Current instruments couldn't find anything about the causes of these changes, research continued on the weave. It was discovered that not everyone is effected by the weave, the other researchers could enter the space unharmed. The weave seemed empty and desire and funding to look into it would be slowed until the hold could be made bigger. 

The gate was widened after 4 years of being open, allowing for the "weave ripper" to be transported into the weave itself. Upon opening another rip within the weave it would create a gateway for instantaneous transportation. 

This discovery lead to the creation of the Weave Gates, the primary FLT travel controlled by [[Derivan Collective]]
